Uks – Empowering Women
Through Media

Changing Perspective, Creating Acceptance

Established in 1997, Uks dedicated itself to the cause of gender
equality, women development and their gender-sensitive
projection on media.

LEARN MORE

OUR VISION

Envisioning a
brighter tomorrow

Ensuring a gender sensitive Pakistan

The impact and power of media in today's electronic age is undeniable. It is an integral part of our lives and plays a vital role in shaping the mind sets of the masses. It is because of this that Uks believes it is imperative that influence of media be harnessed to improve the current status of the women of Pakistan.
