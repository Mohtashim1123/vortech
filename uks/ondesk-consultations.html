On-Desk Consultations

November – December 2013 On-Desk Consultations with Media – visits to media houses and press clubs across Pakistan

Produced by DANIDA

Project - More Women in Media

Participants - 160+

The on-desk consultations were carried out in various media houses across Pakistan. Below are the

details:

Islamabad:

  • Abb Tak
  • Daily Pakistan
  • Express Tribune
  • FM 100
  • Geo News
  • PBC

Peshawar:

  • Aaj News
  • AVT Khyber
  • Express Tribune
  • Frontier Post
  • PBC Peshawar

Quetta:

  • Daily Azadi
  • Daily Mashriq
  • Daily Dawn
  • Baluchistan Times
