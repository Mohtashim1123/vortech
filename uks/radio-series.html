Radio Series

JANUARY 2017

Media Coverage and portrayal of “Honour” Killings in Pakistan

Produced by Nida Naeem, Shaista Yasmeen

Theme - “Honour” Killings

Language - Urdu

Programmes - 1

Funded by Uks Radio Project

A radio programme on the coverage of ‘honour’ crimes by the media in Pakistan was launched with the Annual Desk Diary Publication on the same theme in January 2017. It provided a critical analysis on the trends in the Pakistani media as well as suggestions to counter such gender unjust and unethical practices.

2016

Amn ki Shama sey Ilm ki Lau, (Lighting the Torch of Education through the Candle of Peace)

Produced by Nida Naeem

Theme - Girl’s Education (filter :VAW/GBV)

Language - Urdu

Programmes - 16

Funded by Uks Radio Project

Uks launched a series of 16 radio programmes to show its solidarity with the 16 Days of Activism against Gender-based Violence Campaign 2016. In-line with the year’s theme "FromPeaceinthehometopeaceintheworld:Makeeducationsafeorall!”the radio series focused on the issues inthenationalcontext.The16radioprogrammesfocussedonawidearrayofsocio-economicbarriersingirls’accesstotheirfundamentalhumanright–education.Uks radio producers explored the various factors and gender unjust practices contributing to denial of education to girls including violent extremism, lack of awareness of basic and legal rights, illiteracy, forced marriages, lack of access to opportunities like mobility, economic stability, entrepreneurship etc.

Aurat, Qanoon aur Tahafuz ( Women, Law and Protection)

Produced by Radio producers from KP, Edited and Composed by Nida Naeem and Shaista Yasmeen

Theme - Violence against Women (VAW)(filter :VAW/GBV)

Language - Pushto (15) and Urdu (15

Programmes - 30 radio programmes

Funded by UAAWAZ Fund

Case studies, oral testimonies, interviews of survivors from six districts of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa onprevalence of GBV and the success/positive stories that surfaced from the community interactions with women. The radio programmes included information on the existing legislationon protection of women against violence and international legal instruments to raise awareness of communities on their legal rights on VAW.
