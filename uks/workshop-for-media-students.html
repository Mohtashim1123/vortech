Workshop for Media Students

March 2015 to March 2017 Pakistan-USA Journalists Exchange Program

Funded by U.S. Department of State

Project - Gender Just Media Drive

Participants - 53 participants including faculty and students from various universities.

Trainer/Facilitator -

Wajid Zulqarnain - Associate Professor, SZABIST University

Iffat Ali – Assistant Professor, SZABIST University

The aim of this consultation was to sensitize faculty of media & mass communication departments on how to include the gender sensitization courses in curriculum of various related subjects/fields. It was also focused on spreading awareness about the gender insensitive content in media. And to include Gender Studies as a core course in the subjects of Media and Mass Communication studies.

Names and designations of the faculty members participated in the consultation:

Tehreem Ahmed – Sen. Lecturer, Bahria University Islamabad

Sidra Huma – Lecturer, Islamic International University Islamabad

Ayesha Arif Khan – Asst Professor, NUST University

Zainab Dar – Visiting Faculty, SZABIST University

Shabbir Ahmad Wahgra – Visiting Faculty, SZABIST University

Nirmal Jawed – Lecturer, Riphah University Islamabad

Shehrish Noor – Teaching Fellow, Riphah University Islamabad

Mohammad Ali – Asst Professor, Fatima Jinnah Women University Islamabad

Ayesha Siddiqua – Lecturer, NUML University

Waseem Ghafoor – Lecturer, G.C University Lahore

Dr. Riffat Haque – Associate Professor

Iffat Ali – Asst Professor, SZABIST University

Wajid Zulqarnain – Associate Professor, SZABIST University

Naveed Hashmi – Faculty, SZABIST University
