Daily Print Media Monitoring

Being a Resource Centre, Uks uses the cut-and-paste method to preserve newspaper clippings on all issues concerning women. Ten to Twelve, English and Urdu language daily, weekly and afternoon newspapers are daily scanned with the office bearing a comprehensive record since 1998. This is open to publicwho can make use of it for the purpose of research and analysis. The data is essentially invaluable to the Uks team in facilitating our study of emerging media trends, the shifting balance of gender representation and portrayal of rights issues and concerns.
