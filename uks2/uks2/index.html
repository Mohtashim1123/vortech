Uks – Empowering Women
within and Through the Media

Ensuring gender-responsive media in Pakistan

Uks strives to achieve gender equality and empower all women across various
segments of society through and within the media
y challenging and eliminating the stereotypical and objectifying portrayal and representation of womenin media content.

OUR VISION

Envisioning a
brighter tomorrow

Ensuring a gender sensitive Pakistan

The impact and power of media in today's electronic age is undeniable. It is an integral part of our lives and plays a vital role in shaping the mind sets of the masses. It is because of this that Uks believes it is imperative that influence of media be harnessed to improve the current status of the women of Pakistan.

Recent Projects

Monitoring the media for the last 20 years, Uks has highlightedinnumerable times how media in Pakistan defines and defies gender issues through its content and context. News and current affairs clearly show gender bias as well as blindness, exhibit patriarchal notions, defining certain ‘culturally or traditionally’ sanctioned gender roles.

Beyond Advocacy and Awareness

Calls for a united effort, inclusive of all those involved in the production and presentation of skewed and many a times gender blind content in media, to initiate a conscious, concerted work to produce gender-neutral and gender-sensitive media content.
