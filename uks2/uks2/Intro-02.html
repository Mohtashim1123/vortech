Trainings

Uks has, over the course of time,designedand executed a range of specialized trainings and workshops to different groups within the society. These workshops also include customised and project specific on-desk consultations and round table meetings.We have also partnered with universities and media organisations, within the country and outside, for exchangeprograms and fellowships.
