Publications

We have published and circulatedover 50 publications, including extensive research reports. These help in dissemination of our findings, share our learningsandprovide statistical data on rights issues. These publications not only validate our work but also help to share our milestones in our advocacy efforts for women empowerment. Our work includes detailed reports on media monitoring and content analysisthrough the gender lens on multiple topics that reflect the quality of journalism in our country and pinpoints the areas that need improvement. There are media toolkits that provide gender responsive solutions to complex and sensitive issues through guidelines on reporting and best practices. The annual desk diaries are Uks’ trademark that comprehensively document the state and status of the Pakistani women, on a new theme every year, through the eyes of media since 1947.
